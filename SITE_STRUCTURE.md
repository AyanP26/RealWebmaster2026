# weave.nova - Website Structure & Aspects

This document outlines the functional aspects of the weave.nova platform, detailing its features and information architecture.

### **1. Core Search & Discovery Interface**
*   **Semantic Search Engine**: Uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to interpret user intent and return relevant community resources from Northern Virginia.
*   **AI-Driven Analysis**: Automatically generates "True AI Overviews" (conversational summaries) for search result sets, providing immediate context.
*   **Dynamic Filtering**:
    *   **Requirements**: Quick toggle for "Free Services Only" to assist those in immediate need.
    *   **Categorical**: Hard categorization by service type for structured browsing.
    *   **Geographic**: City and zip-code level destination filtering across the Northern Virginia region.
*   **Interactive Cartography**: Real-time map integration that synchronizes with search results to visually display resource locations.

### **2. Resource Architecture (Key Service Pillars)**
*   **Community & Recreation**: Focus on youth, family, senior services, volunteer opportunities, and recreational programs.
*   **Education & Libraries**: Resources for schools, universities, tutoring, and local libraries.
*   **Food & Basic Needs**: Pantries, shelters, clothing, and emergency food assistance.
*   **Government & Legal**: Support for employment, taxes, immigration, and legal aid services.
*   **Healthcare & Mental Health**: Clinics, therapy, addiction recovery, and medical insurance support.
*   **Restaurants**: Curated directory of local dining, cafes, and specialized cuisine options.

### **3. Information Management & Contribution**
*   **Resource Submission Portal**: A dedicated interface for community members and organizations to contribute new resources to the database.
*   **Verified Database**: Centralized and structured storage of Northern Virginia community resources (JSON-based).

### **4. User Engagement Features**
*   **Search & Interaction History**: A persistent record of previous searches and visited resources, allowing users to easily return to helpful information.
*   **Live Events Board**: A curated section for upcoming local community events such as farmers markets, workshops, and volunteer cleanup days.

### **5. Regional & Accessibility Scope**
*   **Regional Specialization**: Content specifically tailored for Fairfax County, Arlington County, Loudoun County, and Alexandria City.
*   **Bilingual Capability**: Integration to identify and highlight Spanish-language and bilingual services.

### **6. Transparency & Compliance**
*   **Data Usage Governance**: Established Privacy Policy documentation.
*   **Engagement Framework**: Terms of Service and usage guidelines for community interaction.
